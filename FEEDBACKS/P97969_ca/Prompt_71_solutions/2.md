This could lead to slightly more complex processing than strictly necessary if the input were treated as a single continuous string.
*   **Nested Loops:** While functional, the nested `while` loops (one for reading words and another for iterating through characters within a word) add a layer of complexity. A simpler approach might exist that processes the input as a single stream.
